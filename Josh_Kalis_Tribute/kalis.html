Josh Kalis

The skateboarder who just wanted to skate everywhere and progress his skills,
purely for the love of traveling to explore skate urban skate cultures in cities around the world
and the passion for preogression in his skateboarding flows.

Josh kalis is from Grand Rapids, MI but lived in servral cities.

Chicago, Philadelphia, Dallas, San Diego, Barcelona, and San Francisco.

Kalis inspired a lot of skateboarders to keep skating.
